✦Food Spots✦

BUFFET 101

CITY TIME SQUARE, MANDAUE CITY


It is a popular buffet restaurant with a roomy setting ideal for festivities, family get-togethers, or informal meals. It serves a broad range of world cuisines, including Asian, Western, and seafood delicacies.


Open Hours: Lunch: (11:30 AM-3:00 PM) Dinner: (5:30 PM-10:00 PM)

MATIAS BBQ

A.S. FORTUNA STREET, BANILAD, MANDAUE CITY


It is well-known for its tasty and reasonably priced Filipino grilled foods, particularly its famous lechon manok and pig barbecue. It's a popular among residents and tourists who like to savor real Filipino BBQ in a relaxed setting. 


Open Hours: (11:30 AM-10:00 PM), Daily

DELFA'S TUNGOLAN

A.S. FORTUNA STREET, GUIZO, MANDAUE CITY


From the word TUNGOL it means part of the ear. Their famous dishes are insides of pigs. They also sell crispy whole chicken and other varieties if you're not fond of internal organ dishes


Open Hours: Mon - Fri (7:30 AM-11:00 PM)

DIDANG'S DELICIOUS MASAREAL

DOÑA ROSARIO VILLAGE, LABANGON, MANDAUE CITY


It is a beloved local delicacy store, known for its authentic masareal. This iconic spot is a favorite destination for both locals and tourists looking for a taste of Cebu's traditional flavors.


Open Hours: (8:00 AM - 6:00 PM), Daily

MANA ACION'S MASAREAL

346 MENDOZA, MANDAUE CITY


It is a beloved destination for those seeking the iconic sweet and nutty Cebuano delicacy. Known for its homemade quality and authentic flavor, it's a favorite among locals and visitors.


Open Hours: (8:00 AM - 6:00 PM), Daily

LEA MAYOL'S BIBINGKA

A. DEL ROSARIO ST., MANDAUE CITY


Lea Mayol's Bibingka is a local favorite in Mandaue City.. The shop is highly regarded for its consistency and long-standing reputation for serving authentic Filipino treats.


Open Hours: (7:00 AM - 7:00 PM), Daily

BIBINGKA SA MANDAUE

CENTRO, MANDAUE CITY


It is a local spot where you can enjoy freshly made bibingka, perfect for breakfast or a snack. Tt’s a popular destination for both locals and visitors looking to savor authentic Filipino rice cakes.


Open Hours: Mon - Sat (6:00 AM - 5:00 PM)

MANDAUE CITY PUBLIC MARKET

NEAR MANDAUE CITY HALL


A bustling market with over 1,000 stalls selling fresh produce and goods.


Open Hours: 4:00 AM - 9:00 PM

KUYA SIMO'S LARANGAN

KADLUNGAN, MANDAUE CITY


A popular local eatery known for its delicious barbecue and affordable meals. It’s a favorite stop for food lovers in the area.


Open Hours: Monday - Sunday, 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM

NOY GEORGE LARANGAN STATION

M.L.QUEZON ST. CABANCALAN, MANDAUE CITY


It is a local spot known for its laid-back atmosphere and tasty offerings at affordable prices.


Open Hours: (6:00 AM - 10:00 PM), Daily

SOL'S HALO-HALO & REFRESHMENTS

M. L. QUEZON AVE, MANDAUE CITY


A refreshing stop for halo-halo and various desserts, Sol's is known for its delightful treats perfect for cooling off. It’s a favorite among locals and visitors alike.

JOANNA'S HALO-HALO MANDAUE

DOÑA ROSARIO VILLAGE, CORNER 7TH, MANDAUE CITY, 6014 CEBU

✦Culture and Crafts✦

RATTAN FURNITURE HERITAGE



Mandaue’s artisans skillfully create rattan furniture, a tradition passed down through generations, emphasizing quality craftsmanship and sustainability.

BAMBOO ARTISANSHIP



Locals continue the practice of crafting bamboo products, combining functionality with eco-friendly techniques passed through generations.

NATIVE BASKET WEAVING



Basket weaving is a time-honored tradition in Mandaue, with artisans using rattan and native grasses to create intricate, functional designs.

BURI HANDICRAFTS



The practice of making buri palm products, such as mats, hats, and wallets, showcases Mandaue’s commitment to using local, sustainable materials.

ARTISTIC HOME DECOR



Creating home decor from rattan and bamboo is a long-standing practice in Mandaue, where functional pieces are transformed into artistic expressions.
