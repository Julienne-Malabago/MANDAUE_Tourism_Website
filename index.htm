About Us

Mandaue City, founded in 1521, is a historic and vibrant destination known for its rich cultural heritage, scenic waterfronts, and dynamic festivals like the Panagtagbo Festival.

From its origins as a bustling 'Cove of Cebu' to its modern status as an industrial hub, Mandaue offers visitors a unique blend of history, craftsmanship, and Cebuano hospitality.


ATTRACTIONS

Wander Mandaue City's beautifully blend of history and modernity, with historical landmarks like the Mandaue City Hall and National Shrine of Saint Joseph showcasing its cultural heritage.

The city also features vibrant shopping malls, dining spots, entertainment venues, and luxurious hotels, offering a dynamic experience for both locals and visitors.

EVENTS & FESTIVALS

Experience the vibrant energy of Mandaue through its iconic festivals like Fiesta sa Mandaue, where colorful parades and lively street dancing fill the streets, or the Mantawi Festival, a grand celebration of the city's rich heritage and unity. Don't miss the Panagtagbo, a gathering of culture, food, and music that brings the community together in true Mandaue style!

FOOD & CULTURE

Explore Mandaue City, the vibrant heart of Cebu, where rich heritage meets culinary delights like bibingka, masareal, and the iconic lechon.
From historical landmarks to the lively Panagtagbo Festival, the city offers a perfect blend of culture, flavors, and warm hospitality.
